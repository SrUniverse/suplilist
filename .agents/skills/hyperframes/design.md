# Colágeno Hook Video Design

## Visual Direction

**"Dark Luxury Supplement"** — premium health content with energy and movement. Clean typography, vibrant accent colors on dark backgrounds, smooth micro-interactions. TikTok hook aesthetic.

## Palette

| Role | Color | Usage |
|------|-------|-------|
| Background | `#0A0E1A` | Video canvas, scene backgrounds |
| Surface | `#1A1F3A` | Cards, containers, overlays |
| Text Primary | `#FFFFFF` | Headlines, key stats, captions |
| Text Secondary | `#E0E7FF` | Body text, supporting copy |
| Accent 1 | `#FF6B35` | Energy highlight, key metrics, CTAs |
| Accent 2 | `#4F46E5` | Secondary emphasis, underlines |
| Glow | `rgba(255, 107, 53, 0.3)` | Shadows, depth, background glows |

## Typography

- **Headlines**: Poppins Bold 700, 72px–120px (hero), 48px–64px (scene titles)
- **Body**: Inter Regular 400, 24px–32px (captions), 16px–20px (supporting)
- **Data/Stats**: Inter Bold 600, 36px–56px, `font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums`
- **Accent text**: Poppins SemiBold 600, 28px–40px (emphasis)

## Motion Signature

- **Entrance eases**: `power3.out`, `expo.out`, `back.out`
- **Exit eases**: `power2.in`, `sine.in`
- **Stagger**: 100–150ms between elements
- **Hold duration**: 0.4–0.8s per scene
- **Total pacing**: Energy-driven, 60–90 BPM metaphor (fast cuts on hooks)

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Bright accent colors on dark backgrounds for pop
- Micro-animations on text (scale, bounce, slide)
- Glow effects behind key stats
- Varied entrance eases per scene
- Captions tightly synced to narration

**Don't:**
- Flat design — use depth via shadows and glows
- Long holds between scenes — keep energy up
- Muted colors — prioritize contrast
- Multiple fonts per scene — stick to Poppins + Inter
- Static text — always animate in
